FAQs

Can I use Convex for reviewing property contracts from all Australian states?

We are working on it! Currently, Convex is only available to review property contracts from New South Wales (NSW), however we will soon be expanding to other states. Watch this space!

Can I use Convex to review business contracts?

No. Convex is a tool currently designed to review standard property contracts. This service is meant for conveyancers looking to speed up the contract reviewing process before settlement while also increasing the quality of the reviews.

Can Convex review NSW property contracts which are part of strata titles?

Yes! Convex is able to review standard property contracts from NSW including standalone houses units, townhouses, duplexes, and more!

Why should I trust AI with my property contract reviews?

When you use Convex, you are not just a general AI tool, but one specifically built to review property contracts. The tool has been developped by a team of software engineers, lawyers and conveyancers. The tool has been tested meticulously over thousands of contracts before its public debut to ensure the quality of the contract reviews is up to the standard of our team of lawyers and conveyancers. The tool also warns you of any tricky clauses through a quick and easy to understand traffic light system. This makes double checking a clause a breeze!

How long does a contract review take with convex?

A contract review usually takes between 3-5 minutes using Convex. Imagine that? A 100 page contract reviewed in minutes!
